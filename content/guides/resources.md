# Resources

A curated directory of privacy tools, digital security guides, and circumvention
resources. Items marked **[bundled]** are available offline on this removable media.

---

## Privacy & Anonymity Tools

| Resource | License | Notes |
|----------|---------|-------|
| **[Tor Browser](https://www.torproject.org)** | MPL-2.0 | **[bundled]** Anonymizing browser. The foundation of private browsing. [Docs](resources/torproject-manual/) |
| **[Tails OS](https://tails.net)** | GPL-3.0 | **[bundled, M and L]** Live OS. Leaves no trace on host computers. [Docs](resources/tails-doc/) |
| **[OnionShare](https://onionshare.org)** | GPL-3.0 | **[bundled, M and L]** Share files, host websites, and chat over Tor. [Guide](onionshare.md) |
| **[Orbot](https://orbot.app)** | GPL-3.0 | **[bundled]** Tor for Android apps system-wide. [Guide](orbot.md) |
